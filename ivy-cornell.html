Cornell University

Ivy League overview, requirements, focus, and example honors

Major fit and applied learning matter most.

Minimum Requirements (Official)

No official minimum GPA or test score. Policies can change yearly.
  • Common App + Cornell supplement
  • College-specific requirements (Engineering, Arts & Sciences, etc.)
  • High school transcript with strong subject preparation
  • Teacher recommendations (some colleges require specific subjects)
  • Standardized test policy varies by year

What Cornell Typically Values

  • Clear college/major fit
  • Applied learning, projects, or research
  • Strong STEM or subject foundation
  • Initiative and problem-solving

Example Honors / Medals / Awards (Fictional)

  • Honor: Top 10% in National STEM Track
  • Medal: Bronze Medal — Robotics League (2024)
  • Award: Best Engineering Project (2023)
Tip: Match awards to your intended college.
