Harvard University

Ivy League overview, requirements, focus, and example honors

Exceptional depth, leadership, and impact.

Minimum Requirements (Official)

No official minimum GPA or test score. Policies can change yearly.
  • Common App + Harvard supplement
  • Most rigorous transcript possible
  • Strong teacher recommendations
  • Standardized test policy varies by year
  • Optional portfolio (if applicable)

What Harvard Typically Values

  • Exceptional academic achievement
  • Leadership with measurable impact
  • Distinctive strength (national or international level)
  • Intellectual vitality and initiative

Example Honors / Medals / Awards (Fictional)

  • Honor: Top 1% National Exams (2025)
  • Medal: Gold Medal — National Olympiad (2024)
  • Award: National Research Fellowship (2024)
Tip: Harvard admits students with extraordinary depth and impact.
