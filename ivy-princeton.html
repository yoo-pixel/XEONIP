Princeton University

Ivy League overview, requirements, focus, and example honors

Intellectual depth and research readiness.

Minimum Requirements (Official)

No official minimum GPA or test score. Policies can change yearly.
  • Common App + Princeton supplement
  • Rigorous transcript with strong academics
  • Teacher recommendations + counselor report
  • Standardized test policy varies by year

What Princeton Typically Values

  • Deep academic focus and intellectual rigor
  • Independent learning or research
  • Clarity of academic interests
  • Thoughtful writing and reflection

Example Honors / Medals / Awards (Fictional)

  • Honor: School Academic Excellence (Top 2%)
  • Medal: Silver Medal — National Physics Competition
  • Award: Best Research Paper (Regional Symposium)
