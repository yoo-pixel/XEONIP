English Essay Tips

Essay References

Use these sources to learn structure and style. Study them to improve your writing, not to copy.

Books

  • College Essay Essentials — Ethan Sawyer. Clear frameworks and real examples for admissions essays.
  • On Writing Well — William Zinsser. Simple, powerful advice on clarity and style.
  • They Say / I Say — Gerald Graff & Cathy Birkenstein. How to build strong academic arguments.

Websites & Guides

  • Purdue OWL — writing basics and grammar guidance.
  • Harvard College Writing Center — planning, drafting, and revision tips.
  • MIT Admissions Blog — real advice from admissions readers.
  • Stanford Admissions Essay Examples — examples and guidance.

Ethical use: Read examples to learn structure and tone. Never copy sentences or ideas.

Video & Online Resources

  • University writing center YouTube channels (search your target universities).
  • Reputable education channels that explain essay structure with examples.
