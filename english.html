How to Write (Skills)

  • Paragraph structure: topic sentence ‚Üí evidence ‚Üí explanation ‚Üí link.
  • Topic sentences: one clear idea per paragraph.
  • Clarity and flow: short sentences first, then longer ones.
  • Linking ideas: use connectors like ‚Äúhowever‚Äù, ‚Äúas a result‚Äù, ‚Äúfor example‚Äù.
  • Formal vs informal: avoid slang and emojis in academic writing.
Before:

Punctuation & Style

  • Commas: separate ideas in a long sentence.
  • Periods: end one complete idea.
  • Semicolons: connect two related sentences.
  • Capitalization: start sentences and use proper nouns.
  • Paragraph spacing: new idea = new paragraph.
  • Sentence length: mix short and medium sentences.
